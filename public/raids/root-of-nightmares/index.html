Deepsight Resonance Puzzle

Complete three puzzles throughout the raid for a guaranteed Deepsight Resonance weapon after defeating Nezarec. Check seeds (Light/Dark order) at the start, and solve node puzzles in hidden rooms during Opening, Jumping Puzzle, and before Nezarec.

Puzzle 1: After opening, jump through rubble behind the iridescent platform. Shoot the node matching the first seed’s color, connecting it correctly to see "Your Actions Take Root."

Puzzle 2: In the Jumping Puzzle, use the launchpad on a building, turn around, and jump into a floating building to find the second node.

Puzzle 3: Before Nezarec, ascend the large cube, look down, jump to debris below, and enter the room for the final node.

Secret Chests

Secret Chest #1: After Cataclysm, follow the iridescent path left, jump through the door on the right where Scions snipe, defeat enemies, and find the chest with a Tormenter.

Secret Chest #2: In the Jumping Puzzle’s large arena, turn around, look left, shoot the Darkness node below to unlock a door in the back holding the chest.
